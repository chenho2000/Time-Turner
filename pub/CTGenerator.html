Major city time visual representation

this is a widgets to shows the time of the major city in the world. Ex: Toronto, New York, London with a background of the city and the current time. The background will have morning version and the night version to shows the time clearly. User can choose their time showing format by chooing one format from the "Time Format". It will reset all of the cities you have already added. Users can click "reset" button to delete all of the city clock they have already added. They can also double click the clock and delete it. Users are not allowed to add the same city twice. It will be a common use widgets since most of the population in the world live/works in these major cities. They're global centers for finance, innovation and transportation in the world. Almost every web app can put this widgets on their app. Most of the website related to time zone/time will have these major city time on the homepage also.
