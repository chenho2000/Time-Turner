Simple time zone converter

Simple time zone converter utility will create a calendar which the user can enter the date and time, it will shows the dynamic time in the world at corresponding time zone if users choose to not enter the time. Users can choose the format of showing the time. The table will refresh itself. If users entered the time, it will automatically shows all of the converted time in the world. (Note here it will user's current timezone) Users can click "reset" button to reset to current live time.Simple time zone converter will show all of time zone with their current time in a table. It's a useful tool if the web developer want to shows all of the time more clearly at the same time. For example: a flight app may need this utility to shows the time in different time zone country of a flight's take off or landing time.
