Simple StopWatch

The Stopwatch below will record the exact time when you start and the exact time you stop. It will reset after you clicked the reset button.
Please notice, you are not able to click the stop button if the Stopwatch is not started. Also you are not able to click the start button if the StopWatch is running. The correct way of use this stop watch is reset first, click the start button and stop whenever you want. You will get the time period between start and stop. If you want to continue you must notice that the starting time won't change even you stoped a long time.This utility is designed for some developers want to know how fast their users complete a form or complete a test and also record the time period of the day these users doing these forms/tests. The unit will be seconds. (s).
