Major country time visual representation

is similar to the major city one, it will use the capital city of that country as the showing time. User can click on the picture of the country flag and they will see the current time at that country's capital city. The original time will be user's current time.Users can choose the format of showing the time. It will also do the resetting so users need to click on the flag again in order to see the format changes. Users can click on the flag again to see the clock version of the time. And click again will go back to the digital clock.
